Installing Linux

This is a guide to help users transition from their current OS to Linux

Why Switch to Linux?

    Most Linux distros are open source and free

    Retake control of your computer

    Linux distributions are highly customizable to fit your needs

    Revive old computers

Steps to make the switch

Depending on your familiarity with computers, making the switch to Linux can be very intimidating.

That is why I have created this resource to be as detailed as possible to walk users through each step.

While it may be hard to believe, it truly is as simple as following the five steps below.


  1. Back Up Your Data - Backup important files.
  2. Choose a Distribution - Click here for help deciding.
  3. Create a Bootable USB - Download the ISO and use a tool like balenaEtcher to create a bootable USB drive.
  4. Install Linux - Boot from the USB and follow the installation prompts.
  5. Explore Linux - After removing the USB from your computer and restarting, you are now using Linux!
